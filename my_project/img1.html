Bajra Carrot and Onion Paratha


The Dish

This simple recipe is a quick and easy go-to for rounding out your main dishes with a healthy side of vegetables. Leafy kale is sauteed in a little bit of olive oil along with oodles of garlic. Lemon pepper seasoning imparts a nice balance of tangy citrus notes and cracked black pepper. Any dinner would benefit from this addition, be it a creamy pasta dish, grilled meats, stir-fries, or hearty roasts.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. Olive oil
  • 2 cup kale(Chopped)
  • 1 tsp. Garlic(Pilled and Minced)
  • 1/4 tsp. Lemon Pepper Seasoning

Preperation Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the kale and sauté for about 2 minutes, or until slightly wilted.
  2. Add the garlic and lemon pepper seasoning to the skillet, stir to combine with the kale, and continue to sauté for another 2-3 minutes or until the kale is tender.
  3. Remove from the skillet and season to taste with salt and pepper before serving.
