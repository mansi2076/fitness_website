Muffin Mini Pizzas

With Turkey Cheeseburger-Inspired Toppings


The Dish

Indulge that inner child with a tasty lunch (or dinner) that’s big on flavor as well as nutrition. Using better-for-you ingredients makes these little indulgence all the more rewarding. Whole wheat English muffins act as our pizza base, or ‘dough’. An assortment of cheeseburger-inspired toppings are heaped onto our pizza base: lean ground turkey, low-fat shredded mozzarella cheese, sliced red onion, sliced tomatoes, chopped pickles, shredded lettuce, and a light drizzle of yellow mustard for a surprisingly burger-like finish. This is a great lunch option for those busy gotta-get-everything-done weekends.

Time Saving Tips

The ground turkey can be cooked and chilled a day or two in advance, freeing up some time and leaving just the assembly and baking steps. If you’d like to take these mini pizzas to work, assemble them at home, bake them for 3-4 minutes (just to set the ingredients), and cool. Keep the onion, tomato, basil, pickles, and mustard separate, this will help keep your pizzas from getting soggy. When you’re ready for your lunch break, reheat the pizzas in a toaster oven and top with the toppings.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz. whole wheat english muffin(2 Engish Muffins)
  • 4 oz. lean ground turkey
  • ½ cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp. garlic(peeled and minced)
  • ¼ tsp. italian seasoning
  • 1 oz. low fat mozzarella(grated)
  • ¼ cup tomatoes (sliced)
  • ¼ cup lettuce(chopped)
  • 2 Tbsp. pickles (sliced)
  • 2 tsp. red onion (red onion)
  • 2 tsp. yellow mustard
  • 2 tsp. fresh basil (chopped)

Preperation Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F or 200°C. Line a medium baking sheet with aluminum foil and lightly grease with pan release spray.
  2. Lightly grease a small skillet with pan release spray and preheat over medium-high heat.
  3. Once hot, crumble the ground turkey into the skillet and season with garlic, Italian seasoning, and a little pinch of salt and pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until cooked through and nicely browned. Remove from the heat.
  4. Place split English muffins on the prepared baking sheet and top with tomato sauce, spreading the sauce to the edges of the muffin.
  5. Sprinkle the muffins with cheese and cooked ground turkey and place in the oven.
  6. Bake for 5-10 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the muffins are toasted.
  7. Remove from the oven and top mini pizzas with red onion, sliced tomato, fresh basil, and chopped pickles (optional).
