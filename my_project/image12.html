Fruit Salad Side

Fruits to Boost Your Mood and Energy


The Dish

Sometimes you just need a simple side salad of fresh fruit to round out your meal. This classic combination of cantaloupe, banana, strawberries, apples, and oranges is chock full of vibrant vitamins and minerals known to boost your mood and energy. Pick main dishes that need a little fresh fruit boost. We frequently serve this fruit salad alongside our more meaty or protein-focused breakfasts and lunches to provide balance.

Time Saving Tips

Save on prep by purchasing pre-diced fruit. We frequently see grocery stores offering prepared fruit, especially strawberries, cantaloupes, and apple slices. You may find fresh orange segments already prepared. If not, you can use canned mandarin orange slices. Just try to avoid mandarin orange slices that are canned in syrup.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup cantaloupe (diced)
  • ¼ cup banana(sliced)
  • ¼ cup strawberries (sliced)
  • ¼ cup apples(diced)
  • ¼ cup orange segments

Preperation Instructions

  1. Place cantaloupe, banana, strawberries, apples, and orange segments in a serving bowl and toss to combine.
