Grilled Seasonal Summer Veggies

Simple Side for Summer Entrees


The Dish

There’s nothing better than grilled seasonal vegetables. This simple side dish is a perfect pairing for summer mains — grilled meats, pasta dishes, casseroles, etc. Zucchini, yellow squash, broccoli, green beans, tomatoes, and onions are seasoned to bring out the natural flavors of the vegetables — simply a little garlic, salt, and pepper. One trick we use when grilling vegetables is to use a wire rack, placed over the grill grates. This can help keep trickier veggies (green beans and squash slices) from slipping between the grill grates.

Time Saving Tips

To save on time or as a meal prep ahead option, feel free to season, grill, chill, and reheat the veggie. These grilled vegetables are also pretty enjoyable chilled, you could use them as salad toppers or sandwich fillers as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. garlic(peeled and minced)
  • 1 Tbsp. red onion(peeled and diced)
  • ¼ cup zucchini(sliced)
  • ¼ cup yellow squash (sliced)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • ¼ cup green beans
  • ¼ cup tomatoes(sliced)
  • ¼ tsp. black pepper

Preperation Instructions

  1. Preheat a gas grill to medium-high heat or prep charcoals for a charcoal grill.
  2. Place veggies on a large plate and lightly spritz with pan release spray.
  3. Season the veggies with salt (to taste), pepper, and garlic; toss to coat.
  4. Once the grill is hot, place the seasoned veggies on the grill; cook for 4-6 minutes, flipping halfway through, until tender.
  5. Remove the veggies from the grill and serve warm.
