Fruity Smoothie Bowl

With Honey-Oat Dippers


The Dish

This easy, simple, and fruit-forward smoothie bowl adds a little whimsy to your morning routine. The honey-oat dippers are made using whole wheat tortillas. The tortillas are first brushed with honey before being sprinkled with oats and cinnamon and baked until nicely golden and crisp. The smoothie bowl is a combination of tropical fruits, peaches, and bananas blended together with low-fat Greek yogurt for a tasty combination of nutritious ingredients. The fruits are also served on top of the smoothie bowl for a nice, filling texture difference. Spoon or dip 'til your heart's content.

Time Saving Tips

The smoothie bowl mixture can be made well in advance (1-2 days) and refrigerated until ready to top and serve. The honey-oat dippers can also be made in advance, if thoroughly cooled and used within a day or two of baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ oz. whole wheat tortillas (1 each)
  • 2 tsp. honey
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • 2 Tbsp. oats
  • ¾ cup low fat greek yogurt
  • ½ cup frozen peaches (thawed)
  • ½ cup frozen tropical fruit mix (thawed)
  • ¼ cup tropical fruit juice(thawed from the package of frozen fruit)
  • 1 cup banana (sliced and divided)

Preperation Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F or 200°C. Line a medium baking sheet with parchment paper and lightly grease with cooking spray.
  2. Place the whole wheat tortilla on a clean cutting board and cut into wedges.
  3. Transfer the tortilla wedges to the prepared baking sheet, brush with 1 ½ tsp (7.5g) honey, and sprinkle with ½ tsp (.5g) cinnamon and oats.
  4. Place in the oven and bake for 5-8 minutes or until crispy and golden brown. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool.
  5. Place yogurt, half of the peaches, half of the tropical fruit mix, fruit juice, and half of the banana in a food processor and pulse until smooth.
  6. Transfer the smoothie to a serving bowl and top with the remaining peaches, tropical fruit mix, and banana. Drizzle the bowl with remaining honey and sprinkle with remaining cinnamon.
  7. Serve the smoothie bowl with the dippers on the side.
