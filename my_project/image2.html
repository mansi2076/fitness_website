Crispy Cheese Toasties

Tortilla Crisps with Taco Seasoning and Mozzarella


The Dish

When you need a crunchy, cheesy snack or a crispy addition to your meal, you must give these toasties a try. Whole wheat flour tortillas are seasoned with taco seasoning and sprinkled with grated mozzarella cheese and fresh scallions. These toasties bake up crisp on the bottom and cheesy-gooey on top. The combination of taco seasoning and scallions really makes these crisps pop with flavor. These pair perfectly with snack dips and salsas like hummus or pico de gallo. Another great option is to serve these alongside chili, soups, or stews for a little crunchy add-on.

Ingredients

  • 2½ oz. whole wheat tortillas(1 each)
  • ½ oz. mozzarella
  • ¼ tsp. taco seasoning
  • ½ Tbsp. scallions

Preperation Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F or 200°C. Line a medium baking sheet with parchment paper and lightly grease with cooking spray.
  2. Place whole wheat tortilla on a clean cutting board and cut into wedges.
  3. Transfer the tortilla wedges to the prepared baking sheet, lightly spritz the tops of the tortillas with cooking spray, and sprinkle with taco seasoning, mozzarella, and scallions.
  4. Place in the oven and bake for 5-8 minutes or until crispy and golden brown. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool.
